About Us

At DateWarner, we understand that the world of online dating can be both exciting and challenging. While many experiences are positive, unfortunately, some encounters can be less than ideal. That's why we've created a platform where users can report their experiences with individuals they've met on dating apps.

Our mission is to provide a safe and supportive space for individuals to share their stories so we can take care of people who are potentially harmful or cause uncomfortable situations. By sharing your experiences, you can help others navigate the online dating world more confidently.

Whether it's a disrespectful or inappropriate behavior, a concerning encounter, or any other negative experience, your report can make a difference. We take reports seriously and strive to maintain a respectful and inclusive community.

At DateWarner, we believe we can create a safer and more enjoyable online dating experience for everyone.

Thank you for being a part of our community!
